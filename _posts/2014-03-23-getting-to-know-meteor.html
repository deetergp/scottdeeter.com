--- layout: post title: Getting to Know Meteor ---

I was fortunate to have the opportunity to give an introductory talk on the Meteor.js framework at this past Wednesday's FOSS Charlotte Meetup. There were about eight people in attendance, all interested in learning more about what Meteor has to offer. In the talk, I covered an overview of what Meteor is and what guiding principles its creators used, and continue to use, in it's ongoing development.
